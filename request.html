Extended Reading

EXTENDED READING lets you use library resources beyond the service hours of Main Library sections. Meanwhile, EXTENDED CHECK-IN and CHECK-OUT lets you return or borrow books from the Filipiniana Books Section and the Social Sciences and Philosophy Library after 05:00 PM.

Resource on Demand

RESOURCE ON DEMAND (R.O.D.) is a fee-based service for UP and non-UP library users. The ROD service processes articles and book chapter requests from the journals, books, e-resources and other materials available only in UP Diliman Libraries. These documents are scanned and can be picked up in person or sent electronically via email.

Library Orientation/Tour

The Information Services and Instruction Section of the UP Diliman Main Library invites UP Diliman faculty members and their classes to our library orientation/tour program.

Library orientation, which is good for one and half hour class, includes (1) a tour of the library; (2) lecture on the library services offered to the UP community; (3) demonstration on how to use the WebOPAC ; and (4) how to access and maximize the use of online subscriptions of the University.

Audiovisual Room (AVR) Reservation

The Main Library has an Audiovisual Room that can be used for class viewing of multimedia. It is located in the Media Services section at the basement floor.
